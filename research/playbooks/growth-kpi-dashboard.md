# Growth KPI Dashboard

## Purpose

This dashboard defines the key performance indicators (KPIs) used to measure the effectiveness of a LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS companies. It provides a structured approach to tracking awareness, engagement, audience growth, lead generation, and business impact.

---

# Dashboard Overview

| Category | Objective | KPI |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| Awareness | Increase visibility | Impressions |
| Audience Growth | Build a relevant audience | New Followers |
| Engagement | Encourage interaction | Engagement Rate |
| Content Performance | Improve content quality | Saves & Shares |
| Lead Generation | Generate qualified opportunities | Demo Requests |
| Business Impact | Support revenue growth | Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) |

# Dashboard Insights

A successful LinkedIn strategy should not be evaluated by impressions alone. Sustainable growth comes from balancing awareness, meaningful engagement, audience quality, and business outcomes.

Monitoring these KPIs enables marketers to identify successful content patterns, refine their strategy, and continuously improve performance.

---

# Key Takeaways

- Focus on meaningful engagement rather than vanity metrics.
- Measure business outcomes in addition to social media performance.
- Review KPIs regularly and adjust the content strategy based on data.
- Use insights to optimize future content and strengthen long-term growth.
