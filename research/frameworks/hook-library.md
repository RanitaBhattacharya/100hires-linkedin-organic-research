# LinkedIn Hook Library

## Purpose

This document categorizes common opening styles observed across the researched experts. Effective hooks capture attention, encourage readers to continue, and set the direction for the rest of the post.

---

# Curiosity Hooks

Examples:

- Nobody talks about...
- Here's something I wish I had learned sooner...
- Most marketers overlook...

---

# Contrarian Hooks

Examples:

- Stop doing this...
- You're probably measuring the wrong metric...
- More content isn't always better...

---

# Story Hooks

Examples:

- Three years ago...
- My biggest mistake...
- A client recently asked me...

---

# Data Hooks

Examples:

- According to recent research...
- We analyzed...
- Here's what the numbers show...

---

# Question Hooks

Examples:

- What if...
- Have you ever wondered...
- Why do so many companies...

---

# Framework Hooks

Examples:

- A simple three-step framework...
- Here's the process I use...
- My repeatable system...

---

# Reflection

Strong hooks are concise, specific, and immediately relevant to the intended audience.
