# AI-Assisted Research Workflow

## Purpose

This document outlines a structured workflow for conducting marketing research with the support of AI tools. AI is used to organize information, summarize insights, and assist with documentation, while all findings are reviewed and refined by the researcher before inclusion in the repository.

---

# Research Workflow

Identify Research Objective

↓

Select High-Quality Experts

↓

Collect Publicly Available Resources

↓

Review and Analyze Content

↓

Identify Common Themes

↓

Document Key Insights

↓

Organize Research Files

↓

Develop Frameworks & Playbooks

↓

Review for Accuracy and Consistency

↓

Publish to GitHub Repository

---

# AI Support Areas

## Research Assistance

- Organizing publicly available information
- Structuring documentation
- Summarizing recurring themes
- Identifying content patterns

---

## Documentation

- Formatting Markdown files
- Standardizing repository structure
- Drafting templates
- Improving readability

---

## Strategic Analysis

- Comparing marketing approaches
- Extracting reusable frameworks
- Organizing learning resources
- Building playbooks

---

# Human Responsibilities

- Selecting credible experts
- Verifying factual accuracy
- Reviewing AI-generated drafts
- Adding critical thinking and interpretation
- Ensuring originality and proper attribution

---

# Guiding Principles

- Prioritize accuracy over speed.
- Use AI to assist—not replace—human judgment.
- Verify information before publishing.
- Maintain transparency about the research process.
- Respect copyright and intellectual property.

---

# Outcome

By combining structured research methods with AI-assisted organization, the workflow enables efficient documentation while preserving analytical rigor and responsible use of AI.
