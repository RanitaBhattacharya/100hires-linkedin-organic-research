# Content Frameworks for B2B SaaS LinkedIn Marketing

## Purpose

This document summarizes the most common content frameworks identified during the research of leading B2B SaaS marketers. These frameworks help simplify content creation while improving clarity, engagement, and consistency.

---

# Framework 1: Problem → Solution

## Structure

Problem

↓

Why it matters

↓

Solution

↓

Actionable takeaway

## Best For

- Educational posts
- Product positioning
- Marketing tips

---

# Framework 2: Story → Lesson

## Structure

Personal experience

↓

Challenge

↓

Learning

↓

Actionable advice

## Best For

- Personal branding
- Leadership content
- Career growth

---

# Framework 3: Before → After

## Structure

Current situation

↓

Transformation

↓

Result

↓

Key takeaway

## Best For

- Case studies
- Customer success stories
- Process improvements

---

# Framework 4: Myth → Reality

## Structure

Common misconception

↓

Reality

↓

Supporting explanation

↓

Practical recommendation

## Best For

- Thought leadership
- Industry insights
- Educational content

---

# Framework 5: Checklist

## Structure

Objective

↓

Actionable checklist

↓

Summary

## Best For

- Tutorials
- Step-by-step guides
- LinkedIn carousel posts

---

# Key Observation

Across multiple experts, successful LinkedIn content consistently:

- Starts with a strong hook
- Focuses on one core idea
- Uses short paragraphs
- Provides actionable insights
- Ends with a clear takeaway or discussion prompt
