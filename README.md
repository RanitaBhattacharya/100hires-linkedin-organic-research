## Project Objective

This repository contains research on LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS.

The objective is to identify high-performing practitioners, study their content frameworks, analyze their publishing strategies, and organize insights that can later be converted into practical playbooks.

---

## Why This Topic

LinkedIn has become one of the highest ROI organic channels for B2B SaaS companies.

Rather than studying general marketing advice, this research focuses on professionals who actively build audiences, generate demand, and grow businesses through consistent LinkedIn content.
